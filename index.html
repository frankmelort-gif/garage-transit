Your mechanic knows your car. Now your phone does too. Track service history, fuel logs, and get AI-powered advice tailored to your specific vehicle.

What's inside

Everything your car needs

🚗

Multi-Vehicle Garage

Add and manage multiple cars. Scan VIN directly from the plate or registration document using your camera.

🔧

Service History

Log every maintenance record with date, mileage, cost and workshop. Attach receipt photos — OCR reads the total automatically.

✨

AI Mechanic

Describe a symptom and the AI gets your full service history as context. Answers are specific to your car, not generic advice.

⛽

Fuel Log & Efficiency

Track every fill-up, calculate real fuel consumption and compare it to your historical average.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Set alerts by mileage or date — oil change, tire swap, insurance renewal. Syncs directly to your calendar.

📊

Expense Analytics

Visual charts of monthly spending broken down by category. Compare costs across multiple vehicles.

🗺️

Workshop Map

All your service shops and fuel stations pinned on a map based on your records. One tap to navigate.

🔌

OBD-II Connect

Pair a Bluetooth OBD-II adapter to read live mileage and diagnostic trouble codes directly in the app.

Transparency

Why we request access

📷

Camera

Used to scan your VIN from a license plate or registration document, and to photograph service receipts for automatic data extraction.

🖼️

Photo Library

Allows you to attach existing photos to your vehicle or service records, and to save vehicle photos back to your library.

🎙️

Microphone

Used to record voice notes attached to service records and to enable voice input for the AI Mechanic assistant.

📍

Location

Automatically fills in the workshop or fuel station address when creating a new record, and displays them on the map.

📅

Calendar

Creates calendar events for upcoming service dates, insurance expiry and custom reminders so you never miss a deadline.

👤

Contacts

Lets you quickly import your mechanic, insurer or dealer contact details directly into a vehicle profile.

🔵

Bluetooth

Connects to an OBD-II Bluetooth adapter to read current mileage and engine diagnostic codes from your vehicle.

🔒

Face ID

Optional biometric lock to protect access to your garage and vehicle data when you open the app.
